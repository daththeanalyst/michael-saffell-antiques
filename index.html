Our Specialisms

Nearly five decades of expertise in British advertising and decorative antiques.

Biscuit Tins

Tobacco Tins

Advertising Items

Decorative Pieces

Since 1975

Nearly 50 Years of Expertise

Jo and Michael Saffell have been in the antiques trade since 1975. After relocating to Bath, they found the perfect home — a period building with a ready-made shop below, now filled with their remarkable collection of British tins.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or discovering the world of antique tins for the first time, we'd love to share our passion with you.
